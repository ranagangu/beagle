Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles have an incredible sense of smell and tracking instinct.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting hare.
  3. Beagles are friendly and great with kids.
  4. Snoopy from Peanuts is a Beagle.
  5. They are vocal and known for their distinctive howls.
